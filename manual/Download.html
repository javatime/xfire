SNAPSHOT Releases

SNAPSHOT release are from CVS HEAD for the next upcoming release. They often contain bug fixes, but are not guaranteed to work nor will they necessarily have up to date documentation.

xfire-all SNAPSHOTs Jar of all XFire modules (see Dependency Guide)

For Maven 2 users, the snapshot repository is http://snapshots.repository.codehaus.org.
